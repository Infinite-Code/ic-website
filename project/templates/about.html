ABOUT US

Infinite Code is a young, fast-growing software development company with offices in Beijing, China and Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Our clients run the range from large, publicly listed multinationals to young, scrappy startups. Our projects for them all have one common thread though - they are all either mobile web or mobile App based.

 

When asked to define Infinite Code, we point to three core values:

Focus on people and developing them - We believe the greatest determinant of a project or a company’s success is the quality of the people involved. An enormous amount of resources is spent on hiring talented people, developing them and giving them the freedom and support to do their best work. We do our best to create a merit-based, flat organisation structure that allows our people to flourish and excel.
Agile methodologies - Agile methodologies work, period. Our teams are committed to working according to best practices in the Agile methods world. They focus on fast and frequent feature delivery to clients, adapting to changing needs and low overhead communications. All this while also managing to keep the software maintainable and robust.
Open source commitment - Our teams are open source veterans, having used and delivered projects built on open source for years. Where project requirements allow, we prefer to work with open technologies that are collaboratively built by a global group of thousands of software developers. We understand not only how to best utilise them but also the various Free/Open Source Software licenses and their appropriateness for various projects.
 

We do our part as well to support the Open Source community, we donate to the following organisations and/or causes:

The Free Software Foundation (FSF) is a nonprofit with a worldwide mission to promote computer user freedom and to defend the rights of all free software users. Check out the other patrons: https://www.fsf.org/patrons
The Python Software Foundation (PSF) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation that holds the intellectual property rights behind the Python programming language. Check out the other patrons: https://www.python.org/psf/
The Django REST Framework is a powerful and flexible toolkit that makes it easy to build Web APIs with Django. Check out the other supporters: http://www.django-rest-framework.org/topics/kickstarter-announcement/
